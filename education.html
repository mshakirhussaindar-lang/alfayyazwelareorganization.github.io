Empowering Future Generations

At Alfayaz Welfare Trust, we believe every child deserves quality education. Through our Education Support programs, we provide scholarships, school supplies, and digital learning tools to children who need it most. Our aim is to bridge the educational gap and open doors to brighter futures for underprivileged communities.
